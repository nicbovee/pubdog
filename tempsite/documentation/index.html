Passion

Pub Dog Colorado is the first and only restaurant in the state of Colorado, and possibly the nation where it is legal to dine indoors with your dog. Pub Dog Colorado is a restaurant, a craft beer house, and an outdoor dog park all in one. .

Location

The site for this one of a kind destination restaurant is 2207 Bott Avenue on the west side of Colorado Springs, Colorado. This location is 6 blocks from Red Rocks open space, ¾ mile from Bear creek Dog Park, 1.5 miles from world famous Garden of the Gods, and only a few miles from the 1.1 million acre Pike National Forest. We are close enough to all places that you love taking you dog so why not make it a day out and visit Pub Dog Colorado!

About us

Dine Indoors With Your Dog.

Pub Dog Colorado has spent 2 1/2 years to get approved through the Health Department, receive a zoning change to C5, purchase the property, and finalize construction plans. We have been financed and will be breaking ground in June of 2016. Colorado is in the top three dog friendly states in the country, and Colorado Springs is in the top 5 cities in the country for dog friendly lifestyles. We take our dogs with us everywhere. The vast open space on the west side of Colorado Springs sees millions of visitors per year with as much as 50% using these parks with their dogs. Pub Dog Colorado will give these outdoors minded people a place to eat and enjoy local craft brewers while not having the guilt of leaving their dog in the car. With over 300 days of sunshine per year, the west side of Colorado Springs is the epicenter for outdoor activity. Our guests can enjoy our 1,200 SF south facing patio at all times of the year. If the weather is not co-operating, our guests can come into the restaurant with their dogs. Our 3,000 SF fenced outdoor area adjacent to the patio allows our clients to enjoy food and drink in an area of K9 grass that allows your dog to play off leash with other dogs while you enjoy your time with other like-minded dog lovers. Once built, we dog lovers will finally have a place to socialize with other dog loves over good food and drink. We look forward to getting this destination restaurant open for all to enjoy.

Colorado Craft Brewers

Colorado has over 160 craft brewers and continues to grow. Colorado Springs has quickly become the mecca of craft brewers on the front range of Colorado. Pub Dog Colorado will allow our clients to enjoy 10 different brewers product on tap from one place. There is nothing as satisfying as an ice cold craft brew on tap after a day in the local outdoor open spaces. Cheers!
